Description

DelegatePal is a website co-founded by me to help students taking part in MUN conferences prepare for their roles. It provides easy access to information scraped from various sources to allow users to search for data relevant to what they need. It evolved from MUNme into my Personal Project, which was eventually remade into the current version of DelegatePal. Later, I made a mobile app for it which has been installed nearly 4000 times. We have partnered with reputed MUN organizations such as Best Delegate, THIMUN, UN4MUN and MUN Impact to further the cause of MUN internationally.
